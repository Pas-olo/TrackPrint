GLOBAL STATS
HOW TO FIND DISTANCES

Not sure what distance to enter? Here are some tips by transport type.

✈️ Plane

Use the great-circle distance (straight line between airports). Tools like airmilescalculator.com give you the exact distance between any two airports.

🚆 Train · 🚌 Bus · 🚗 Car · 🚴 Bike · ⛵ Boat

Use the actual route distance. brouter.damsy.net is a great tool that calculates precise itinerary distances for all these modes. Google Maps or any navigation can app works too.

METHODOLOGY

How are CO2e emissions calculated?

All factors are in kgCO2 equivalent per km and include upstream emissions (extraction, refining, transport). Radiative forcing is included where relevant.

✈️ Plane — DEFRA 2025

Radiative forcing already included in all factors. Short-haul (<3,700 km): Economy 0.126 kgCO2e/km · Business 0.189 kgCO2e/km (no Premium Eco or First on short-haul — redirected to Economy/Business). Long-haul: Economy 0.117 kgCO2e/km · Premium Eco 0.187 kgCO2e/km · Business 0.339 kgCO2e/km · First 0.468 kgCO2e/km.

🚆 Train — DEFRA 2025

Single international average factor: 0.00446 kgCO2e/km.

🚗 Car — DEFRA 2025

Thermal FE: 3.1 kgCO2e/L (average fuel, upstream included). Consumption by type: Sedan 7L · Hybrid 4L · SUV 9L · Van 10L · Motorcycle 4L per 100km. Total trip emissions divided by number of passengers.

Electric vehicles use consumption (Small EV 15 kWh per 100km · SUV EV 22 kWh per 100km · Van EV 30 kWh per 100km) × the electricity emission factor of the country recorded for that day. Fallback: world average 0.471 kgCO2e/kWh (Ember).

🚌 Bus

Per-passenger factors: Coach 0.030 kgCO2e/km · Sleeper 0.080 kgCO2e/km · City bus 0.100 kgCO2e/km · Minibus 0.050 kgCO2e/km.

⛵ Boat — DEFRA 2025

Foot passenger: 0.01871 kgCO2e/km · Car passenger (with vehicle): 0.12933 kgCO2e/km.

🚴 Cycling / 🚶 Walking

Considered carbon-neutral — 0 kgCO2e.

🔖 Other

Uses your custom emission factor (kgCO2e/km). Must be a valid positive number, otherwise 0.

Sources: DEFRA 2025 Greenhouse Gas Conversion Factors · Ember Global Electricity Review 2024 · Our World in Data — consumption-based CO2 emissions per capita, 2023

ABOUT

TrackPrint

A personal travel diary to track the countries you visit, the distances you travel, and the carbon footprint of your journeys.

Data is stored locally on your device and never sent to any server.

Borders, flags, and map data are based on available source files and librairies, and do not reflect any political stance or militant viewpoint.
